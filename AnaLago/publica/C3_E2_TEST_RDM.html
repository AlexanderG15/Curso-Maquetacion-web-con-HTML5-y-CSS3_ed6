About Roller Derby Madrid

Roller Derby Madrid was formed in 2012 by a group of women determined to bring the sport of roller derby to their city. Since then, they have played a host of national and international teams, both at home and away. The league is made up of members who work hard to make it the best league it can be, promoting teamwork and supportive competition among sportswomen.
We became a WFTDA Apprentice League in 2015 and founded our B team in 2016: two of our proudest achievements to date.
